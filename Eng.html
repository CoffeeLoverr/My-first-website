About me

Thank you for visiting my website. I'm Anna, an interpreter, translator and English tutor.

My main mission is to streamline communication and ease it for parties involved.

I believe that interpretation is done well if nobody notices that there was any, and training is efficient if after each class a student feels more at ease in the new language environment.
